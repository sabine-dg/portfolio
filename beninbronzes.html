The Theft and Disunification of the Benin Bronzes

This story map delves into the British Colonial Military Campaign on Benin and the thousands of objects stolen. I am passtionate about archaeology and it is imperative that any archaeologist critically approach museums and the story of every artifact, which often encompasses violence and theft. Using data from Digital Benin, I combine quantitative spatial data with critical cultural analysis in order to understand how colonialism has shaped the circulation and ownership of archaeological heritage.
